Péter Aranyosi

Comedian

His career:

Péter György Aranyosi (Miskolc, July 19, 1972 -) Hungarian humorist with a Karinthy ring. He is a member of the Dumaszínház and a regular performer of the Showder Club.

He studied at the Gergely Berzeviczy Secondary School of Commerce and Hospitality in Miskolc, and then at the Teacher Training College in Nyíregyháza. He was an employee of the Rádiókabaré and then joined the Esti Showder, working as the head of the Design Center section when the show started. He was also the audience organizer of the Mónika Show, and then he started performing at the Godot Dumaszínház.

Family:

He is married and has two children.

Prizes:

  • Karinthy ring (2017)
  • Muse of Miskolc Award (2018)
